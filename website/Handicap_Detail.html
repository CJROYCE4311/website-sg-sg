About This Audit

The Handicap Committee provides this report to ensure fair play and transparency for all members. By comparing your Course Handicap Used against each round's Score Differential, we can identify rounds that materially outperformed the number used for net scoring.

The "Probability" Column: This metric estimates the statistical odds of producing a round at the displayed differential gap using the course handicap bucket shown in the table. For example, consistently producing "1 in 1,000" rounds may suggest the course handicap used is too generous.
