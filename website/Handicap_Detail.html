About This Audit

The Handicap Committee provides this report to ensure fair play and transparency for all members. By comparing your Current Index against an Implied Index (calculated from your recent tournament scores), we can identify potential discrepancies.

The "Probability" Column: This new metric estimates the statistical odds of a player shooting their recent scores given their current index. For example, consistently shooting "1 in 1,000" odds scores may suggest the Current Index is higher than the player's potential ability.
