About This Audit

The Handicap Committee provides this report to ensure fair play and transparency for all members. By comparing your Current Course Handicap against an Implied Differential (calculated from your recent tournament scores), we can identify potential discrepancies.

The "Probability" Column: This metric estimates the statistical odds of a player reaching the displayed adjustment using their current course handicap bucket. For example, consistently producing "1 in 1,000" adjustments may suggest the player's current course handicap is too generous.
